about me

Hello world, my name is Asfir Sukhran. I was born in Volgograd(formerly Stalingrad) on 28 July 1941, just a couple of days after the first world war started. My labor occurred at a tend on the war field while all areas were under missile attack. Luckily I survived the war with my parents and 2 brothers (another brother was born a couple of years later). My father, Reuven, influenced me the most in my childhood. Reuven served in the Soviet Union army as conductor of one of the most prominent orchestras. He was a man of great value, and because of his service, we were transported every couple of months or years to another location, so most of my childhood I spent traveling with my family. After finishing school, I worked at an electronic manufactory and participated in their handball team. In one game I played, a referee didn't take his eyes off me and only gave fault for my benefit. When the game ended, I thanked him for being so nice to me, and he told me that instead of thanking me come with me on a date, and this man AvrahamVoshinski became my future husband. I did "Eliha" to Israel in 1993 to Yeruham and have lived here since then. Today I have two beautiful daughters, four grandchildren, and one great-grandchild. My main hobbies are reading books, listening and singing to music, and mostly being in nature.
